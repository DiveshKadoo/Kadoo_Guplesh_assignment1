Visa Requirements

If you need an invitation letter to support your Visa application in order to attend the conference please send an email to srrnet-conf-2021@udm.ac.muand include the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Passport Number and Country Issued
  • Full postal address
  • Email address
  • Mobile Number
  • Expected duration of stay in Mauritius

All submitted information will be verified prior to the letter being produced and all invitation letters will be sent by email. Please note that the invitation letter does not guarantee that you will receive a visa.

You will find important information on the Passport and Immigration Office, Mauritius website.
